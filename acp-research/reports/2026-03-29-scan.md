### #1672 — Agent Identity Verification for Agent Cards (2026-03-29) 🔥 ACP已领先
> "No protocol-level mechanism to verify who it is communicating with... relies on transport-layer trust (HTTPS)"

### #1667 — Heartbeat-based agents: offline-first task handling (2026-03-29) 💡 ACP有部分解法
> "Agents that run on a schedule (every 4 hours via cron)... A2A assumes a live service endpoint"

**ACP 状态：🟡 部分覆盖**
- ACP 有离线消息队列（`ERR_NOT_CONNECTED` + offline delivery queue）
- 但没有显式的 `availability_window` 字段（"我每4小时上线一次"的声明机制）
- **潜力特性**：可在 AgentCard 里加 `schedule: {type: "cron", expr: "0 */4 * * *"}` 字段

---

### #1029 — Publish/Subscribe for async communications (2026-03-28) 💡 值得关注
> "Support pub/sub methods for async communications — fan-out to multiple subscribers"

**ACP 状态：🔴 未覆盖**

- 但 SSE `/stream` 已支持多 subscriber
- **潜力特性**：`/topic/<name>/subscribe` + `/topic/<name>/publish` — 1-to-N fan-out
