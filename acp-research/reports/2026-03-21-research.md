## Relevance to ACP

### `failed_msg_id` (ANP 99806f45)
Our v0.9 already echoes `failed_message_id` in error envelopes (`ERR_TIMEOUT`, `ERR_MSG_TOO_LARGE`).
ANP is converging on the same pattern for E2EE errors. ✅ We are ahead.

### `client_msg_id` + `server_seq` (ANP 1f0abd2d)
ACP already ships `message_id` (client-generated) and `server_seq` (server-assigned sequence).
Both match ANP's approach. ✅ No gap.

### `failed_msg_id` in error scope
**Potential improvement:** We currently only emit `failed_message_id` for timeout/size errors.
ANP emits it for ALL error types. Consider broadening to include `ERR_INVALID_REQUEST`
and `ERR_NOT_CONNECTED` in v1.1.
